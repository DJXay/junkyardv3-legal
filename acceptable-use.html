Acceptable Use Policy & Seller Guidelines

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

To maintain a secure and trustworthy marketplace, all users listing items on Absolute Junkyard / JunkyardList must adhere strictly to this Acceptable Use Policy. Failure to do so will result in the immediate removal of listings and permanent suspension of account access without a refund of listing fees.

1. Prohibited Items and Listings

You are strictly prohibited from listing, advertising, trading, or selling any of the following items on the Platform:

  • Stolen property or property without a verifiable legal title where applicable.
  • Counterfeit or replica parts mislabeled as original equipment manufacturer (OEM) components.
  • Hazardous chemicals, industrial waste, un-drained fuel tanks, spilled battery acids, or volatile explosive components.
  • Weapons, ammunition, fireworks, or regulated firearms accessories.
  • Recalled equipment or machinery flagged by the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) as dangerous.
  • Any items or services that do not relate broadly to automotive, lawn care, industrial, mechanical equipment, tools, or machinery parts.

2. Listing Accuracy and Fraudulent Description

Sellers must provide true, complete, and accurate descriptions and depictions of the items they list. You may not:

  • Use stock images or stolen photographs that do not accurately represent the exact physical condition of the item in your possession.
  • Misrepresent the compatibility, performance, model year, make, or operating state of machinery.
  • Engage in deceptive pricing practices or bait-and-switch advertising tactics.

3. Reporting Violations

If you discover a listing that violates this policy, or if you suspect a user of engaging in fraudulent behavior, please document the item URL and report the activity manually to our monitoring team at: [Insert Abuse/Reporting Email, e.g., abuse@yourdomain.com]. We review notices systematically and reserve the right to remove content immediately to preserve platform safety.

Violations of this policy result in immediate listing removal and permanent account suspension with no refund of listing fees.

4. AUTOMATED ENFORCEMENT AND SYSTEM FLAG RULES

We utilize an automated AI safety flagging layer to scan listings for restricted, hazardous, or prohibited items upon upload.

If your listing contains technical terms, descriptions, or imagery matching prohibited categories (including but not limited to un-drained fuel tanks, hazardous materials, or missing title declarations), your listing will automatically be funneled into a hidden moderation queue for administrative review.
