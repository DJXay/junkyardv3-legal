Dispute, Refund & Escrow Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

Absolute Junkyard / JunkyardList / Junkdrawlist operates exclusively as an introductory venue and advertising space for independent buyers and sellers of lawn care and automotive equipment parts. Because we are not a party to the transactions executed between users, the following policies apply:

1. No Platform Escrow or Intermediation

The Platform does not hold funds, offer financial escrow services, process transactional payments between buyers and sellers, or guarantee shipping, delivery, or pickup. All financial handovers and product exchanges happen directly between users offline or via independent third-party payment methods. Users assume 100% of the risk associated with exchanging funds and goods.

2. No User Transaction Mediation

The Company does not act as a judge, arbitrator, mediator, or referee in disputes between buyers and sellers regarding item condition, non-delivery, description mismatches, or quality. If you experience a dispute regarding an item purchased or sold on the Platform, you must resolve that dispute directly with the other party.

3. Refund Policy for Listing Fees

The Platform charges independent fees to sellers for the privilege of creating and hosting a public listing.

  • All listing fees paid to the Platform are strictly non-refundable.
  • Listing fees are not contingent upon the item selling successfully.
  • If a listing is removed by the platform due to a violation of the Acceptable Use Policy, or if a user account is suspended due to fraud or misconduct, any and all prepaid listing fees associated with that account are immediately forfeited and will not be refunded.
By paying a listing fee and submitting a listing, you acknowledge and accept that the fee is non-refundable under all circumstances.
